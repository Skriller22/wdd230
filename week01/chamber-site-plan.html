Site Name: Jezero Crater Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Purpose:

The Jezero Crater Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to making our historic home on Mars the planet's center of commerce. Were here to help you carve your name in history, and make your mark on the red planet.

Target Audiences:

  • Tourists: Access to historical landmarks, the excitement of visiting another planet, exploring natural wonders, scientific marvels, migration offices.
  • Businesses: Access to a new market, a new workforce, and a new frontier.
  • Settlers: Career oppourtunites, local company information, scientific and industrial oppourtunities, bio-sciences, a local university.

Scenarios:

  • Businesses: The chamber will host regular councils about the economic status of the city, and have an event afterwards with job oppourtunities and company liasons.
  • Settlers: Can join regular public forums and have 24 hour access to the immigration offices.
  • Tourists: People who access the site from earth or mars can have access to local travel agencies and events in the city.
